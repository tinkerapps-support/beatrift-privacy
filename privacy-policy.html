Privacy Policy

Beatrift  ·  Last updated: July 4, 2026

Overview

Beatrift is a mobile rhythm-puzzle game developed by Tinker Apps. Tinker Apps does not operate its own backend servers and does not intentionally store personal data on infrastructure controlled by Tinker Apps. However, certain third-party services integrated into the app (such as Google services) process data on our behalf or as independent data controllers, as described in this policy. Tinker Apps does not sell personal data. All Google services referenced in this policy operate under Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Controller

The data controller responsible for this app is Tinker Apps (United States). You can contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com.

Data We Collect

Tinker Apps does not operate its own backend servers and does not intentionally store personal data on infrastructure it controls. Your game progress — including Shards, level progress, Forge upgrades, resonance skins, sound packs, boosts, jar collections, and settings — is stored locally on your device using standard application storage.

Cloud save (optional). If you choose to sign in to Google Play Games, your game progress is additionally backed up to the Google Play Games Saved Games service in your Google account, so it can follow you to a new device or survive a reinstall (see Google Play Games Services below). This backup is stored by Google, not by Tinker Apps. If you do not sign in, progress exists only on your device: uninstalling the app or clearing its data will lose it (non-consumable purchases can still be restored through Google Play — see below). Game data is designed not to include directly identifying personal information.

Google AdMob

Beatrift displays rewarded and interstitial ads served by Google AdMob. If an ad is shown, AdMob may independently collect:

  • Device identifiers, including the Android Advertising ID
  • IP address and approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

This data is collected by AdMob and shared with Google and its advertising partners for ad targeting and measurement. AdMob processes this data according to Google's privacy practices and policies; Tinker Apps does not independently access or use this advertising data outside of the reports and controls Google provides. You can opt out of personalised advertising and limit certain ad-related data collection in your device settings under Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalisation. AdMob is operated by Google under Google's Privacy Policy. For details of how Google uses information — including IP addresses — from sites and apps that use its services, see How Google uses information from sites or apps that use its services.

Ads in the EEA, UK & Switzerland

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or Switzerland, Beatrift uses a Google-certified Consent Management Platform (Google's User Messaging Platform) to present a consent request before ads are personalised. Your choices determine how ads are served:

  • If you consent, Google and its advertising partners may use your advertising identifier and related data to show personalised ads.
  • If you do not consent, ads are served as non-personalised — not targeted using behavioural or personalised advertising profiles. Google may still process limited contextual, security, fraud-prevention, and operational data necessary to deliver ads and to comply with applicable law.

Changing or withdrawing consent. You can review and change your choices at any time — withdrawing consent is as easy as giving it — from within the game under Settings > Manage Ad Privacy Options. Withdrawing consent does not remove ads; it switches them to non-personalised. These controls are shown where required by applicable law.

Google Play Billing & In-App Purchases

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Google Play. Tinker Apps does not receive or store any payment information. All purchase validation is handled by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Beatrift offers the following types of in-app purchases:

  • Shard bundles — consumable, one-time grants of in-game currency. Consumable purchases cannot be restored through Google Play. However, your Shard balance is part of your game progress: if you are signed in to Google Play Games, it is carried over to a new device or reinstall via cloud save. If you are not signed in, Shards are lost together with local game data.
  • Remove Ads — a non-consumable purchase that disables interstitial ads (optional rewarded ads remain available). It is linked to your Google Play account and restores automatically on reinstall.
  • Resonance Pack — a non-consumable purchase that unlocks premium cosmetic skins and a sound pack. These cosmetic items are linked to your Google Play account and restore automatically on reinstall.

If you uninstall and reinstall the app, non-consumable purchases (Remove Ads and the Resonance Pack) are restored automatically via your Google Play account. Consumable Shard grants are not restored by Google Play, but are recovered as part of your game progress via cloud save if you are signed in to Google Play Games; otherwise they are not recoverable if game data is deleted or lost.

Google Play Games Services

Beatrift optionally integrates with Google Play Games Services for sign-in, leaderboards, achievements, and cloud save. Beatrift does not create or maintain its own user accounts; signing in uses your existing Google account, which is created and managed by Google. Sign-in is optional — if you do not sign in to Google Play Games, Play Games profile data is not shared and no game progress is backed up to the cloud. However, other integrated Google services (such as AdMob and Firebase) may still process data as described elsewhere in this policy.

If you do sign in, Google Play Games Services independently collects the following for app functionality purposes:

  • User ID (Gamer ID) — a unique identifier linking your Google account to your in-game profile
  • App activity — achievement and leaderboard progress used to power Google Play Games features
  • Saved game data (cloud save) — a snapshot of your game progress (Shards, level progress, Forge upgrades, cosmetics, boosts, gameplay statistics, and purchase entitlements such as Remove Ads) stored in the Google Play Games Saved Games service in your Google account, so your progress can be restored on a new device or after a reinstall. Saved game data does not contain payment information or directly identifying personal information.

Beatrift uses Google Play Games Services solely to provide sign-in, leaderboards, achievements, and cloud save. Tinker Apps does not store this information on its own servers or use it for any other purpose. Google Play Games Services operates as an independent data controller under Google's Privacy Policy.

Firebase Analytics

Beatrift uses Google Firebase Analytics to understand how players interact with the game. Firebase Analytics may automatically collect:

  • App events (e.g. session start, first open, in-app purchases)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language)
  • A randomly-generated app instance ID
  • Approximate location derived from IP address

This data is processed by Google under Google's Privacy Policy. Tinker Apps uses analytics reports and aggregated insights generated from this data to understand gameplay trends, improve the app, and evaluate features. Tinker Apps does not use Firebase Analytics to directly identify individual users. In the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, Firebase Analytics does not store or use device identifiers until you consent through Google's consent message; if consent is not given, analytics identifiers remain disabled (Analytics may still record limited, non-identifying information needed for basic operation). You may also be able to limit certain data collection through your device or Google privacy settings.

Firebase Crashlytics

Beatrift uses Google Firebase Crashlytics to automatically detect and report app crashes and errors. When a crash occurs, Crashlytics may collect:

  • Stack traces and crash logs
  • Device state at the time of the crash (device model, OS version, available memory)
  • Installation and diagnostic identifiers used by Firebase Crashlytics to associate crash reports with a particular app installation

This data is used solely to diagnose and fix bugs and is not used to identify individual users. Crashlytics is operated by Google under Google's Privacy Policy.

Age Signals & Age-Appropriate Experiences

To support applicable U.S. state app-store age-verification laws (such as the Texas App Store Accountability Act, SB 2420), Beatrift uses the Google Play Age Signals API. Where required by law in your region, Google Play may verify a user's age and provide the app with an age category (for example, an age range such as 0–12, 13–15, 16–17, or 18+) and parental-consent or supervision status.

Beatrift uses this information only to provide an age-appropriate experience — specifically, to apply more restrictive, non-personalised advertising settings for users who are minors, and to restrict in-app purchases for minors without verified parental consent — and for legal compliance and safety, as permitted by these laws. Tinker Apps does not use this age information for any other purpose and does not store it on its own servers. Age verification and parental consent are handled by Google Play. This signal is provided only for users in regions where it is legally required; in all other cases the app applies its standard settings.

Data Retention

Tinker Apps does not retain personal data on its own servers. Retention periods for data processed by Google services are governed by Google's own policies:

  • AdMob — ad interaction and identifier data is retained per Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Firebase Analytics — event-level data is retained for a limited period configured within Firebase (subject to Google's defaults, which may change from time to time); aggregate data may be retained for longer.
  • Firebase Crashlytics — crash reports are retained for a limited period set by Google.
  • Google Play Games Services — achievement, leaderboard, and saved-game (cloud save) data is retained in your Google account until you delete it via your Google account.

Local game data is retained on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data in your device settings.

Data Deletion

Tinker Apps does not store your data on its own infrastructure. To manage or remove data:

  • Local game data — uninstall the app or clear app data in your device settings (you can also use the in-game Settings > Reset Progress option). If you are signed in to Google Play Games, using Reset Progress while online also updates your cloud save to the reset state; a cloud copy may otherwise remain in your Google account (see below).
  • Achievement, leaderboard, and cloud save data — delete via your Google Play Games profile or Google Account settings under Settings > Delete Play Games account and data. This also deletes saved-game (cloud save) data.
  • AdMob data — managed by Google; visit myaccount.google.com.
  • Firebase Analytics & Crashlytics data — processed by Google. Some data may be associated with device- or app-level identifiers rather than your Google Account and may not be individually accessible or deletable by Tinker Apps. For information about Google's privacy controls, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Some data collected by third-party services (such as Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics) may not be individually identifiable or deletable, but is used in aggregate and retained according to Google's data retention policies.

To request deletion of data associated with your use of Beatrift, or for help with any of the steps above, contact us at tinkerapps.support@gmail.com. We will respond to and assist with deletion requests where reasonably possible, or direct you to the appropriate Google privacy controls.

Your Choices

You have several controls over the data processed through the app:

  • Withdraw or change ad/analytics consent (EEA, UK & Switzerland) — open Settings > Manage Ad Privacy Options in the app to re-present the Google consent message.
  • Opt out of personalised ads / reset your advertising ID — in your device settings under Settings > Google > Ads.
  • Delete achievement, leaderboard, and cloud save data — via your Google Play Games profile or Google Account settings under Settings > Delete Play Games account and data.
  • Manage data held by Google — at myaccount.google.com.
  • Remove local game data — uninstall the app, clear app data in your device settings, or use the in-game Settings > Reset Progress option (see Data Deletion for how this interacts with cloud save).

Security

Tinker Apps does not store personal data on its own servers. Local game data is protected by Android's standard application sandboxing. All data transmitted between the app and the integrated Google services (AdMob, Firebase, Play Games, Play Billing) is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS), and data handled by those services is subject to Google's security practices. No method of electronic transmission is completely secure.

Legal Basis for Processing

Tinker Apps does not intentionally collect or store personal data on its own servers, but does determine how certain third-party services are configured within the app. Where we act as a data controller in that capacity, we rely on the following legal bases under GDPR and equivalent laws:

  • Legitimate interests (Firebase Crashlytics) — Crashlytics is used to maintain app stability and security. Crash data is not used by Tinker Apps to directly identify users by name, email, or similar directly identifying information.
  • Firebase Analytics — in the EEA, UK, and Switzerland, analytics that rely on device identifiers run only after you consent through Google's consent message; until you consent, and if you decline, analytics storage stays disabled. Outside those regions, Firebase Analytics is processed under our legitimate interests in understanding aggregate app performance and improving gameplay. Analytics data is used only in aggregate and is not used to identify individual users.
  • Consent (EEA, UK & Switzerland) — personalised advertising relies on your consent, gathered through Google's certified consent platform. Where consent is not given, ads are limited to non-personalised serving. You may withdraw consent at any time under Settings > Manage Ad Privacy Options.

For data processed directly by Google services, Google acts as an independent data controller and relies on its own legal bases as described in Google's Privacy Policy.
